Usage
Basic Alert: 
<div class="alert alert-primary" role="alert">
    A simple primary alert
</div>
Dismissible Alert: 
<div class="alert alert-warning alert-dismissible fade show" role="alert">
    Alert content
    <button type="button" class="btn-close" ng-click="closeAlert()"></button>
</div>
Available Classes:
  • .alert-primary, .alert-secondary, .alert-success
  • .alert-danger, .alert-warning, .alert-info
  • .alert-light, .alert-dark
  • .alert-dismissible - Makes alert dismissible
  • .alert-link - Styles links within alerts
